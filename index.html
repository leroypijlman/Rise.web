WHY RISE?

During your work, you spend too much time on searching for the right documents, articles and pages. Seriously...

Information worker does not mean Information Searcher

Why should you spend your valuable time in searching for the right documents, articles, pages or any type of content you instantly need? The truth is that figures show that professionals spend a hefty amount of time only for searching for the right documents. That is not where humans are made for. Let machines give you the right content.

There is no document search system

Off course, Rise is also a Search Engine. But it is designed to be used as short as possible. Users do not have to learn how to use a search engine built by Rise. The role of the search engine should be as tiny as possible in order to get you to move on with your work. It is not about searching for the right document. It is about inspiring you to move on.

Let Rise learn about the documents, you focus on the job

Documents and data are to support your job. Your job should not be a quest to search for these documents, neither is to study what is inside thos documents. Due to the Artificial Intellince aspect, Rise is able to learn about the context of documents. This way Rise is able to inspire you to consider only the documents that are useful for your current task at hand.

FAST

Users will not be slowed down. There is no "search" button. Results appear as you type and dissappear when you clean the search box. Even for large volumes (e.g. > 100.000) of documents, no lags or heaps are experienced.

ORGANIZED

Documents will be indexed in a structured fashion. This makes searching back for those documents more easy and accurate. This way you get a document storage solution for free along with the search engine.

INTELLIGENT

The A.I. features make Rise learn from your data. This way it understands to context to help users by inspiring them with keywords that are related to the topic the user is looking for. This knowledge is totally based on the documents that are imported. This way the suggestions of Rise are totally based on the context of your organization.
