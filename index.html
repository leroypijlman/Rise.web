Rise is the fast, A.I. empowered legal information retrieval platform. The role of the search engine should be as tiny as possible. It is not about searching for the right document. It is about providing information and inspiration you need so you can focus on high level tasks.

INTELLIGENT

The A.I. features allow Rise to learn from the context whithin the legal documents. This way it understands and helps users by inspiring them with suggested keywords that are related to the topic the user is looking for.

ORGANIZED

Documents will be indexed in a structured fashion. This makes searching back for those documents more easy and accurate. This way you get a document storage solution for free along with the search engine.

FAST

Users will not be slowed down. There is no "search" button. Results appear as you type and disappear when you clear the search box. Even for large volumes of documents, no lags are experienced.
